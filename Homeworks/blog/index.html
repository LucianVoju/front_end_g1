Hi, I’m Lucian.

my photo

I’m a young entrepreneur, employee, father, amateur fisherman and now back to student. My writing skills are awful so I tend to copy and paste stuff that sound good from the web.

I was born in the great city of Cluj Napoca in 1984. After finishing George Cosbuc High School and FSEGA University I got a job at Germanos Chains of Stores, that was in 2004. In 2012 I moved to Romtelecom,Telekom today, and in 2013 together with a friend of mine launched propertybook.ro, this is the entrepreneur part.

Propertybook is a kind of real estate search engine and I have no idea how to write a single line of code on it. Kind of late, in may 2017 I decided to go back to school and learn new stuff. This new stuff is called Front-end Web Development.

The Mission:after 5 months of intense learning I will have all the knowledge to get a new job as a “web developer” and hopefully in one year I will be able to implement new features on my webpage 😃
